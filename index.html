About

The story of our family owned business.

  • 2022-2023

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Everything started with four high school best friends that had a dream to own and operate a service company to help millions obtain the services services and products they needed. They worked hard in the door to door industry to save up the capital to fulfill that dream.

  • October 2023

    An Agency is Born

    After working out legal protections, getting proper licensing, receiving help and mentorship from experts, and establishing our mission our company started to take shape!

  • May 2024

    Transition to Full Service

    We officially earned our first real customer and we were officially a fully operational company operating out of Utah. Since then our customer base grew quickly and before we knew it we had reached 20,000 customers!

  • April 2025

    Phase Two Expansion

    After our company grew too large to manage out of one single office, we expanded to three other offices across the country with an office on both the west and east coast as well as another in the mid west.
